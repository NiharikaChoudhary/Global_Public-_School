    School Functions

    School functions are an integral part of school curriculum. Several functions are held every year. These functions include Annual Prize Distribution, Annual Sports Day, Investiture Ceremony, Founder Father’s Birthday, Republic Day, Independence Day, Teacher’s Day, Children’s Day, School Exhibition, School Fete etc. A rich flavour of music, dance and orchestra are the highlights of our functions. School exhibition unfolds the potential of students. It helps the students to imbibe the value of hard work and unleash their own creativity. The spirit of ’I can do’ channelizes one’s energies and helps one to set a goal in life. The school appears as a colourful mini museum conceiving all sorts of variety in itself. These school functions manifest practical implication of knowledge and provide a suitable platform to the students to prove their latent talents.
